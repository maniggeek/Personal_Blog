ABOUT ME

I’m MANI, a 28 year old geek and programmer living in Iran with my family and my wonderful friends.I primarily read literary Si-Fi and Fantasy. Spend free times at my local indie bookstore, and love my friends circle. If I’m not reading I’m probably watching a conceptual movie, developing a new program, drinking coffee in a Cafe , or practicing a new language. I started this blog in early of 2021 as a way to share Literary Lifestyle Content and Geek Culture. Going forward, I’ll be making my way through my unread shelf, exploring the literary community around me,and of course continuing to recommend all the best books are important for Geek Community. You can see much more over on Instagram and send email through maniggeek@gmail.com Cheers, friends!
